kaffe. v.0.0.2

Kaffe is Danish for—you guessed it—coffee. And I love kaffe. After working in the beverage industry for nearly a decade, I switched careers and became a software developer. Kaffe, this app, is the merging of those passions.

Kaffe is a coffee-lover's companion and a way for me to share & codify (get it?) my knowledge of selecting, roasting, brewing, and enjoying coffee. v0.0.1 started out as nothing more than a timer and brewing instructions for Tetsu Kasuya's 4:6 method, but as Kaffe continues to grow, so does my knowlege and love of coffee and code.
